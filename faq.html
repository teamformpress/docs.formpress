Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about FormPress.

Is FormPress intended to replace other form plugins?

No. FormPress is designed as an alternative form builder with a focus on simplicity and extensibility.

Can FormPress be extended later?

Yes. FormPress is built to allow additional features and integrations to be added in future versions.

Does FormPress send data to external services?

No. By default, FormPress does not send submitted data to external servers.
